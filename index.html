My Specialized Services

Cloud Security

I design and manage secure cloud infrastructures on AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, focusing on: Securing environments with compliance frameworks. Conducting vulnerability assessments and threat detection. Automating security in CI/CD pipelines. Ensuring data protection with encryption, IAM, and secure backups. DevOps

I deliver end-to-end DevOps solutions focused on automation, scalability, and reliability. Monitoring system performance and troubleshooting issues to minimize downtime. I focus on maintaining reliable, secure, and high-performing systems to support business continuity and growth.
